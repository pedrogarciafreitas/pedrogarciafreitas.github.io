> `<br />
# wget http://downloads.sourceforge.net/project/htop/htop/1.0.1/htop-1.0.1.tar.gz<br />
# tar -xvf htop-1.0.1.tar.gz<br />
# cd htop-1.0.1/<br />
# ./configure --prefix=/usr/local/bin<br />
# make<br />
# make install`
